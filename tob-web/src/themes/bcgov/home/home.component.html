Search for information on legal entities in British Columbia.

Currently, you can find BC Registry incorporation information for 1.3 million companies. This information includes legal names, "doing business as" names and registration status.

Providing easy access to this information increases trust within the business community, and reduces the friction for businesses to apply for other permits and licences.

Additional information will be added in the future.
