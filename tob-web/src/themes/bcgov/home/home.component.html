home.about-title

B.C.'s OrgBook is a searchable public directory of open verifiable data about B.C. legal entities.

This new service uses cutting-edge blockchain-based technology, which provides the foundation to help B.C. move to a digital economy.

The OrgBook supports our goal to streamline and modernize services for British Columbians as part of our broader Digital Government Strategy.

Right now, people can use B.C.'s OrgBook to quickly verify if a company is registered to do business in B.C.

In the future, verifiable data such as permits, licenses, and other accreditations will be added to B.C.'s OrgBook.
